About Me

I'm a software architect and engineering manager with over 25 years of hands-on experience building enterprise-grade systems and leading the teams that power them. My career spans the full arc of modern software — from the early days of the web through cloud-native, microservices, and AI-augmented development.

I bring a rare combination of deep technical depth and organizational leadership, having served as both an individual contributor architect and an executive-level engineering manager. I'm equally comfortable whiteboarding system designs, reviewing pull requests, and presenting roadmaps to stakeholders.

My focus is on building durable systems and growing the engineers who maintain them — cultures where quality, pragmatism, and continuous learning thrive.
